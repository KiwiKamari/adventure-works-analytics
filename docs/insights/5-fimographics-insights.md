## Revenue Structure & Business Dependency

The combined firmographic and profitability analysis suggests that business performance depends heavily on specific customer groups, industries, and geographic regions.

### Key Findings

- Revenue dependency is concentrated across particular B2B segments.
- Certain industries appear strategically more valuable than others.
- Geographic and customer concentration may introduce operational risk exposure.
